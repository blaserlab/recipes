# Pronase

For dechorionating zebrafish embryos.

## Ingredients 

Below are the ingredients and the amounts required to make a 1 mg/mL working stock of Pronase.


| **Amount** 	| **Item** 			|
|---------------|-------------------------------|
| 50 mg		| Pronase 			| 
| 50 mL		| E3 medium 			|

## Directions

1.  Combine the above ingredients.  E3 should be without methylene blue.
2.  Mix until dissolved.
3.  Store at -20C in 1 mL aliquots.
