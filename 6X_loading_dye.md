# 6x loading dye

## Ingredients

Below are the ingredients for the 6X working stock solution.

| **Amount**   	| **Item** 	|
|---------------|---------------|
| 2 mL 		| 50X TAE 	|
| 0.15 g 	| Orange G 	|
| 60 mL		| Glycerol 	| 
| to 100 mL 	| Milli-Q water |

## Directions

1.  Combine all ingredients.
2.  Stir until dissolved.
3.  Filter if necessary.
